Online Gameshow

We organized a first-of-its-kind online gameshow - a fun activity for our community to get together virtually and participate in a game show. This was very successful with 30 teams participating and overall 10 shows conducted, each show running for 2 - 3 hours. The semi-finals and finals were live-streamed on facebook and kept several viewers glued to the screens!
