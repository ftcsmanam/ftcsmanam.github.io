FTCS Manam for Kids

We want to have a place where kids can come together and hangout with each other while doing some good activities under adults supervision. A few activities we are currently conducting include practicing Srimad Bhagavadgita, learning about Ganesha, Hanuman and slokas.

We would like to help in the personality development of our kids by teaching them good habits, values, social responsibility, self control, etc., We really appreciate all the help we can get from our community.
