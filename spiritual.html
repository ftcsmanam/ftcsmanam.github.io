FTCS Manam - Spirituality

A study group and satsang devised to be an instrument in our spiritual journey, inspiring each other, sharing divine thoughts and enriching our knowledge and understanding of Sanatana Dharma.

In the process, we are planning on doing a systematic study. We will take up a few topics and read them over time and understand it. We can share our understanding and opinions here in this group about that topic. If any of your friends share similar interest please invite them to join in our group.

For the topics, we will start with 3 topics. We will do them simultaneously.

  1. Prasnottara Ratnamaalika by Adi Sankaraacharya
  2. Srimad Bhagavadgita
  3. Srimad Bhagavatham by Veda Vyasa

Plan is... you can find material regarding the topic and study, understand and share your thoughts. We can help each other all the way. But it would be nice if you could take some time and study. At least half hour to one hour in a week.

If you would like to participate, please contact us.
